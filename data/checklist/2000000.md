Start with 1 kettlebell 8-15kg, advance to 2.

## Variations

### Variation A
Strength focus
Circuit - Repeat 3x

#### Squat
8-12 reps
Rest 60 seconds
Goblin squat, one kettlebell

#### Clean and press
6-12 reps
Rest 60 seconds
Alternate arms, one kettlebell

#### Kettlebell rows
10-15 reps
Rest 90 seconds
Alternate arms, one kettlebell

---

### Variation B
Repeat 6x
Conditioning and power focus

#### Kettlebell swings
15-20 reps
Rest until end of minute

#### Kettlebell thruster
5-8 reps
rest until end of minute
